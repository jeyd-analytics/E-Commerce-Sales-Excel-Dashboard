# 🛍️ E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive E-commerce Sales Dashboard** built using Excel, aimed at analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and the impact of discounts on profitability.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into **actionable business insights**, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which product categories are the most profitable?
* Which city has the highest orders, sales, and profit?
* Who are the top 3 most frequent buyers and what did they purchase?
* Which subcategories generate the highest order volume and revenue?
* How do discounts impact profit and purchasing behaviour?
* How do sales vary across months or seasons?


## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** ₹533.67M
* **Total Profit:** ₹79.71M
* **Total Orders:** 5000
* **Average Order Value:** ₹106,733


### 🔹 Visualizations Included

* 📍 **Sales by Region** – Identifies top-performing regions
* 💰 **Profit by Category** – Highlights most profitable categories
* 🌍 **City-wise Performance** – Orders, sales, and profit distribution
* 👥 **Top Customers Analysis** – Identifies frequent buyers
* 📦 **Subcategory Performance** – Sales and order trends across products
* 📉 **Discount vs Profit Analysis** – Shows impact of discounting strategy
* 📈 **Monthly Sales Trend** – Identifies seasonality patterns


### 🔹 Interactivity

* Dynamic slicers for:

  * Region
  * Category
  * Sub-category
  * City
* Cross-filtering across all visuals
* Clean and structured layout for easy navigation


## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and structured before analysis:

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Standardized category and sub-category names
* Converted date formats
* Created derived columns (Month, Year, Discount Categories)


## 🧠 Key Insights

* The **West region** contributes the highest sales among all regions
* Certain categories drive **higher profit margins despite lower volume**
* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total orders
* **Higher discounts are associated with reduced profitability**
* Sales show **seasonal fluctuations**, indicating demand patterns
